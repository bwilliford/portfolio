Florida Power & Light

Florida Power & Light is a utility company in south Florida known for having one of the most reliable electricity grids in the United States. They are also a very forward-thinking utility company with respect to their web application and customer experience.

While working with Fjord in 2015-2016, FPL contracted us to redesign their entire application to be cleaner and more consistent. I specifically worked on the "Report Power Outage" and "Bill Pay" experiences.

OBJECTIVES

  • Allow FPL users to pay their bills from their phone or other device, as well as enroll in automatic bill payment and budget billing
