Platform for Connecting Changemakers

Ubuntoo is a startup founded by former CocaCola executives who want to make a positive change in the world. It is a platform for finding sustainable environmental solutions, and their initial focus is primarily ending plastic pollution.

This was a very rewarding client to work with because of their noble mission. They brought me on board to help with UI design and visual design as the platform had already been built out for the most part by their development partner Oneture, but they needed it to look more consistent, modern, and clean as well as responsive.

MY ROLE
  • UI Design
  • Visual Design
  • CSS
OBJECTIVES
  • Establish a style guide and consisent branding / visual design across platform
  • Improve layout of various pages and ensure pages would display properly on mobile (responsive)
  • Design new pages including user profile, payment, news, and membership options
KEY RESULTS
  • Platform was launched in 2019
  • 750+ partners including major brands like CocaCola and Clorox
  • 1500+ sustainability solutions for ending plastic on the platform

ENSURING RESPONSIVE DESIGN
