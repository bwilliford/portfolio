South Inc. is a young marketing company in Nashville, TN. They approached me for branding services as well as a new website. They were thrilled with my design and both the brand and website remain today more than 3 years later.

BRANDING IDEATION

The client was a young company and was going for a hip, vintage look which is popular in graphic design today. They knew they wanted there to be some element of a compass in there and the "south" direction so that was enough to work with.

FINAL DESIGN

The client chose a direction and it formed the basis for a compelling brand and web presence which continues to this day.
