MORE THOUGHTS ON ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

When you are thinking about engagement you are really thinking about motivation - what motivates people to stay engaged with an app?

Different people have different motivations.

Motivation framework from Jon Radoff (2011)

Achievement

- Badges, achievements, levels, progression

- Daily or weekly missions (e.g., “Place 3 bets on underdogs” or “Win 5 parlays in a row”) with rewards.

- Loyalty Rewards Reach certain earnings levels/thresholds like silver, gold, platinum

Competition

- Earnings Leaderboard (Local and National)

- Friend View See what others in your social network are betting on, or your local community (e.g. Austin)

Cooperation

- Betting Leagues with Friends: Allow users to form private groups and compete on betting success.

- Live Chat & Forums: Create spaces for users to discuss bets, strategies, and upcoming games.

Immersion

- Sports feeds - Key moments or select games

- Spin-the-Wheel or Loot Boxes: Allow users to win free bets, odds boosts, or exclusive content by logging in daily.
