I'M A BRIDGE BETWEEN
DESIGN AND ENGINEERING

I'm a multi-disciplinary designer and computer scientist that's passionate about designing and building interactive experiences that empower people.

I have been making clients and employers happy for 10 years with top-notch design solutions, clear communication, and leadership.

My research is at the crossroads of HCI and AI, with applications in educational technology, sketch-based interfaces, and creativity support. I’m passionate about designing and building software that helps people learn, grow, and become more creative. I believe technology can empower people to learn at their own pace and even accelerate learning.

When I’m not absorbed in design, development, or research, I am likely playing music, traveling, or playing video games – A great source of design inspiration.
