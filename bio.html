I bring technology to people in a form they can love and understand

I'm a multi-disciplinary designer and creative technologist that's passionate about designing and prototyping interactive experiences that empower people and organizations.

I like to define design as "bringing technology to people in a form that they can love and understand". I'm passionate about designing and building simple, usable, and beautiful products and services.

Because of my PhD background, I am also a specialist in prototyping and building highly custom and unique UIs including ones that leverage AI. If it can be imagined, I'm confident I can build it.

When I'm not absorbed in design, I am likely playing music, drawing, or traveling.
